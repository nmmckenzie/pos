All of the boxes on this page have default (static) positioning.
  • absolute items are all positioned relative to their parent's 0,0 (top, left). siblings (blue and yellow) will stack.
  • if box1(black) is display: inline, top: 10px, it moves within its parent
  • it's possible to have blue fall below yellow and its children if it's given a top:**px value large enough
  • it's possible for content in a level1/wrapper to push down past the next sibling and interfere with content of siblings and niblings.
  • the black and purple boxes have the same dimensions
